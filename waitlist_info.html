How it Works

ExperienceMe helps local experience businesses be discovered without changing how they take bookings. Businesses create listings, users explore experiences, and bookings stay on the business’s own website.

1
Businesses create experiences

Businesses add a title, description, location, pricing, and images so customers can understand the experience at a glance.

2
Admin review (quality check)

Submissions are reviewed to keep listings accurate, safe, and consistent across the platform.

3
Users browse and discover

Customers explore experiences by location, category, and budget, with tools like the Experience Finder to narrow options.

4
Bookings stay on the business’s own site

When someone is ready, they click through to the business’s existing website or booking tool. Businesses keep control of payments, availability, and communication.

5
Measure what’s working

Businesses can view simple metrics such as views, saves, and booking link clicks to understand interest and refine future listings.

ExperienceMe is onboarding businesses gradually ahead of launch.
